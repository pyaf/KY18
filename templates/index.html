About US

Kashiyatra, the annual cultural festival of IIT BHU Varanasi is a three day fiesta, aimed towards mesmerizing everyone with enchanting literary, musical and artistic events. Its grandeur has earned it the fame of being north India's largest cultural festival. IIT BHU rests amidst the sanctity of Asia's biggest residential university, BHU in the holy city of Varanasi. Varanasi, the eternal city, houses a plethora of traditions and has an ecstatic blend of cultures of myriad races. IIT Bhu has been nationally acclaimed for imparting technical knowledge in diverse fields and IIT BHU will be celebrating its cetenary in a couple of years.
