  • About us

    Kashiyatra, the Annual Socio-Cultural festival of IIT (BHU), Varanasi is an enchanting blend of various events of music, dance, fine arts, fashion, literature, theatre and quiz moulded into a three-day fiesta of monumental proportions. Being North India’s biggest cultural festival, year after year it flaunts a higher participation with the count being more than 300 colleges last year and an ever growing fan base. Kashiyatra not only offers a perfect platform for those who wish to stage their talent and art nurtured in them, but also is known for its most sought after pronites. The pronites have created their own brand by the performances of leading artists like Sachin-Jigar & Nikhil D'souza, EDM by Sunburn, Anish Sood & VH1 Supersonic and famous bands like The Local Train, Euphoria, Tiny Fingers, Demonic Resurrection & Coke Studio raising the expectations of Kashiyatra to a new level with every edition.
    The city "Varanasi", which hosts the grandeur of this fest is another invaluable gem studded in the jewel of Kashiyatra and is a major element which pulls the crowd towards itself by its heritage, spirituality, cuisine and hospitality.
